[Midjourney Quick Start Guide]


1. Go to Midjourney.com, select Join the Beta, or go directly to the Midjourney Discord.

Before joining the Midjourney Discord server, you must have a Discord login.

2. Find a Newbies Channel

3. Trial users can make roughly 25 Jobs before needing to subscribe. A Job is any action that uses the Midjourney Bot.
